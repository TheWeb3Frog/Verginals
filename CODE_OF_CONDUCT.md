# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
Verginals community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional
  setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
maintainers at **abuse@verginals.com**.
