Meet the Alpha Verginals

This is history: the very first collection of native inscriptions ever created on the Verge (XVG) blockchain. 3,333 hand-crafted pixel cats, each carrying a Verge rune, each written directly onto the chain: no server, no database, no middleman. Once yours is inscribed, it lives on Verge forever.

Every Verginal belongs to one of three Houses: Fire, Water and Earth, with 1,111 in each. You don't choose which one you get: a provably-fair random draw seals your Verginal the instant you start, and reveals it the moment your payment confirms. Here's a taste of what's inside the vault:

Our vision

Verginals exists to celebrate what Verge has always stood for: a free, fast, community-owned blockchain that belongs to no one and to everyone. We are builders who love this network, and we wanted to give the community a brand-new way to play, create and express itself on it.

Decentralization first

We are deeply attached to decentralization and to Verge's founding vision. Verginals is fully self-service and non-custodial: your inscription is written straight onto the public Verge blockchain and sent to an address you control. There is no account, no gatekeeper, and no central party that owns your Verginal. The blockchain is the source of truth, not us.

Built by the community, for the community

Verginals was created independently, outside of the Verge core team. We are not the Verge Core developers and we do not speak for them. We built this because we believe inscriptions can add fun to Verge and open up new ways for the community to express itself and experiment on-chain.

At the same time, we hold Verge and its contributors in the highest respect. We are open to ongoing, permanent dialogue with the Verge core team and the wider community, so this project can grow calmly, transparently and in harmony with the network it lives on. If anything we do can be done better for Verge, we want to hear it.

Inscriptions are forever: inscribe with intention

Please read this carefully. Everything you inscribe is written permanently and publicly onto the Verge blockchain. It cannot be edited, hidden or deleted by anyone, including us. There is no undo. Keep this in mind and never inscribe private, sensitive, personal, confidential or unlawful information. Treat every inscription as public and eternal, because that is exactly what it is.

Where we're headed

The Alpha Verginals are only the beginning, the first native inscriptions collection ever created on Verge. Our hope is that this becomes a lasting, community-driven playground on XVG. We'll keep listening, keep it fun, and keep it aligned with the spirit of Verge.
